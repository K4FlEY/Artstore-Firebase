Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this website legit?

We Dreamy Art store are 100% legit and we are working to make this websites user data as secure as possible.

2. How can I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our collection of arts, click on the desired item, and then click the "Buy" button. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

3. Is it safe to make online payments on your website?

Yes, your online payments are secure. We use encrypted payment gateways to ensure the safety of your transactions.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the shipping carrier's site.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal for online payments. Visit our "Payment Options" page for more details.

6. Can I return or exchange a purchased item?

Yes, we have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Please review our "Returns and Exchanges" page for detailed information on the process.

7. How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

8. Does our private information get stolen by the website

No,We dont sell or share our users personal information.
